About UoEmCU

The University of Embu Christian Union (UoEmCU) is an interdenominational, student-led body of Christian students at UoEm. We are united by a common faith in the Triune God (Father, Son, and Holy Spirit) and a heavenly calling according to Holy Scriptures.

As a non-profit and non-political organization, our core mission is to make disciples of Jesus Christ within the campus community, embracing our rich interdenominational, ethnic, and gender diversities.

Our Story

Founded with Vision

UoEmCU was established with a clear purpose: to create a space where students from all denominations could unite in their common faith, grow spiritually, and make a lasting impact on campus.

Growing in Faith

Over the years, UoEmCU has grown from a small group of committed believers into a thriving community of over 3,000 members. This growth reflects our commitment to discipleship and authentic fellowship.

Making an Impact

Today, we continue transforming lives through Christ-centered discipleship, vibrant worship, and meaningful service. Every student has a unique calling and a role to play in God's kingdom.

Looking to the Future

As we move forward, we remain committed to making disciples of Jesus Christ, embracing diversity, and creating lasting spiritual transformation for generations to come.

Our Vision

To create a thriving community of Christian disciples at the University of Embu who are committed to spiritual growth, authentic fellowship, and transforming the campus through Christ-centered leadership.

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ within the campus community through evangelism, discipleship, equipping believers for service, and promoting unity across denominational boundaries.

Our Core Values

01

Faith in Christ

We believe in Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior and commit to building our lives on His teachings and the Gospel message.

02

Spiritual Growth

We prioritize personal and collective spiritual development through prayer, Bible study, and accountability in our faith journey.

03

Unity & Community

We celebrate our diversity and seek unity across all denominational, ethnic, and social boundaries in one body of Christ.

04

Service & Compassion

We demonstrate Christ's love through acts of service, care for the vulnerable, and commitment to social justice on campus.

Our Members

Just as the body has many members, find your unique place in the UoEmCU family.

Full Member

Fully committed disciples actively participating in all CU activities, events, and spiritual growth opportunities with voting rights in organizational decisions.

  • Full voting rights
  • Leadership opportunities
  • Spiritual mentorship
  • All exclusive benefits

Special Member

Students committed to the CU vision with active participation but may be transitioning or have specific roles within the community.

  • Event participation
  • Community access
  • Growth programs
  • Mentorship support

Associates

Friends interested in exploring the CU community, attending events, and learning more about our faith and mission.

  • Public events
  • Community gatherings
  • Explore our mission
  • Join anytime
